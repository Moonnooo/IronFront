## Commercial licensing (IronFront)

IronFront is available under a **public non-commercial license** (see `LICENSE`).

If you want to use IronFront for any **commercial purpose** (including selling, reselling,
licensing, bundling with paid products/services, or distributing for a fee), you must obtain
a **separate commercial license** from the copyright holder.

This includes monetized content creation (ads/sponsorships/affiliate links/paid access) or
accepting donations/tips related to IronFront.

### How to request a commercial license

- Open an issue in this repository with the title: **Commercial license request**
- Include:
  - Your name / company
  - Intended use (what you’re building / selling)
  - Distribution model (one-time sale, subscription, bundles, etc.)
  - Expected volume (rough estimate)

The copyright holder will respond with terms and pricing.
